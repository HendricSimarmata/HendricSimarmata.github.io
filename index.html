About Me

Hello!

I am a Freelance Graphic Design specializing in portraits and cartooning with a style that mixes new age digital techniques. My others skills are Create Logo as Branding Identity and Digital Panting. With over 3 years of experience in the multi-media field.

My goal is to provide professional and high quality design for the client satisfaction. I like this job and want to build up a higher career in this profession. I am seeking opportunities to continue growing my experience. Client satisfaction and quality work are my top priorities.

If you have a project that you'd like to work together on, please don't hesitate to get in touch.
